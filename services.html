Our Services

Part Load (LTL)

In our LTL service, material is collected from the customer’s unit at their given time and then our company hub consolidates material with other cargo and moves it further. While clubbing the LTL material with other cargo, utmost care and attention is paid to the nature of goods so that the material being transported does not get damaged. The most important feature of our LTL service is that the material is made to arrive at final destination using only one transshipment which is at its point of origin. We load the material in same vehicle at the very first hub in which it will get door delivered to the client at the destination. In cases, where material is over 5 ton, we pick up and deliver the LTL material in same vehicle, and add other material from our hub, resulting in zero transshipment for our customer.

Full Truck Load (FTL)

Full truckload, FTL is a type of shipping mode whereby a truck carries one dedicated shipment. In other words, the journey is reserved for one shipment only. FTL trucking has several advantages over the alternative trucking shipment mode, LTL, or less than a full truckload shipment.

Door to Door Delivery

Door-to-Door (D2D) is a shipping method where freight is picked up at the door of the sender and delivered to the recipient’s door. The shipment is collected from the sender and taken directly to the recipient.

Cash on Delivery

Cash on delivery (COD) is a payment term that requires the recipient to pay for goods at the time of delivery. The payment is usually made in cash, but the seller may also accept a check. The shipping company collects the payment and sends it to the seller.
